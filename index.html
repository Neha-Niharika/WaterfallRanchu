The Waterfalls of Ranchi
"The earth has music for those who listen." - William Shakespeare

The mighty waterfalls of Ranchi,
A sight to behold, a wonder to see.
Their roaring cascades and misty sprays,
Bring a sense of awe and serenity.

Like veils of nature cascading down,
Creating a painting in motion so grand.
The beauty and power of these waterfalls,
Are a testament to nature's wondrous hand.

Ranchi is blessed with numerous waterfalls and dams that are a treat for nature lovers. Here's a list of some of the popular ones:

  • Hundru Falls: Located about 45 km from Ranchi, Hundru Falls is one of the most famous waterfalls in Jharkhand. It cascades from a height of around 98 meters and offers a spectacular view of the gorge below.
  • Jonha Falls: Also known as Gautamdhara Falls, Jonha Falls is located about 40 km from Ranchi. The waterfall is surrounded by lush green forests and the water drops from a height of around 43 meters.
  • Dassam Falls: Located about 40 km from Ranchi, Dassam Falls is known for its scenic beauty and crystal clear water. The waterfall is surrounded by dense forests and offers a breathtaking view.
  • Hirni Falls: Hirni Falls is located about 70 km from Ranchi and is surrounded by lush green forests. The waterfall drops from a height of around 37 meters and is a popular picnic spot.
  • Panchghagh Falls: Located about 55 km from Ranchi, Panchghagh Falls is a five-tiered waterfall and is known for its picturesque beauty.

Dams:

  • Rukka Dam: Rukka Dam is located about 34 km from Ranchi and is a popular picnic spot. The dam is built on the Subarnarekha River and offers a serene and calm environment.
  • Kanke Dam: Located about 7 km from Ranchi, Kanke Dam is built on the Kanke River and is a popular spot for boating and picnics.
